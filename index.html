About Alejandro Sanz

Alejandro Sanz is a Spanish singer-songwriter and musician from Madrid, celebrated for his poetic lyrics and flamenco-influenced pop. With a career spanning more than three decades, he has earned numerous awards including multiple Latin Grammy and Grammy Awards, and is best known for hits such as "Corazón Partío" and "Amiga Mía".

Renowned for his heartfelt performances and collaborations with international artists, Alejandro has built a devoted global fanbase. This official website is the place for fans to connect, get updates on new music, tours, exclusive content, and special fan experiences.
